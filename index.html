projects

MPs on Twitter

(mpsontwitter.co.uk)

Website which archives tweets and presents aggregated data about the use of Twitter by UK MPs.

Also includes the Twitter account and Twitter bot @UKMPtweets

Data collected through this project has helped a variety of research papers, including: Community Security Trust, CARR (Centre for analysis of risk and regulation at LSE), and Bloomberg's QuickTake section.

UK News Index

(uknewsindex.co.uk)

Website analysing which news articles are being shared most by Twitter users.

Created as part of my Master's degree, part of an exploration into the relationship between social media sharing and information dissemination.

Wiki Live

A live dashboard of edits to Wikipedia articles.

Created as part of my Master's degree, observing how user interaction can be analysed to discover trending or important topics.
